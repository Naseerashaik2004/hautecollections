About us

Matsya was born in 2014. The thought of Matsya was not that of a brand,
it was story, a journey. I wanted to set on a journey to evolve a new lifestyle,
a life that took the old & the new together. Royalty in its most minimalistic
form, evolved for the urban life, simplicity beyond imagination. We wanted
to create a taste in luxury that was accessible, that didn’t feel untouchable.
A sort of grounded exquisite grandeur, a contrast of extremism in a very real
form.
